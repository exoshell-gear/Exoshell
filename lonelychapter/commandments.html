The Ten
Commandments

Savage wisdom for merchants, operators, and builders. The things nobody tells you until you've already learned them the hard way.

· · ·
I

Commandment I

Thou shalt test many offers, for the first one will probably not work.

II

Commandment II

Thou shalt not worship a single ad, for even the best creative eventually dies.

III

Commandment III

Thou shalt respect the data, and not thy own ego when the metrics say thy offer sucks.

IV

Commandment IV

Remember the customer, and keep thy promises honest — they are watching everything.

V

Commandment V

Honor thy email list, for it will make thee money when ads grow expensive.

VI

Commandment VI

Thou shalt not panic during red days, for tomorrow may bring profitable ROAS.

VII

Commandment VII

Thou shalt not steal creatives without improving them, lest thy ads perform worse.

VIII

Commandment VIII

Thou shalt keep testing, for winners are found through iteration, not hope.

IX

Commandment IX

Thou shalt build a brand, so thy business is not just another forgettable name in a crowded market.

X

Commandment X

Thou shalt not quit too early, for the breakthrough often appears after many failures.
